In ruby, one can write multiple codes to remove multiple blank spaces inside strings (sentenses) 
 
"Write      your       string            here   ".squeeze.strip
"This     is my      input  string     ".gsub(/ +/, ' ')
"Write      your       string          here   ".split('  ').join(' ')
